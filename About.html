About Us

ThinkSys, a global technology products & services company, helps customers improve and grow their business and e-commerce initiatives across the Web and mobile channels. ThinkSys develops, tests and implements high-quality, cost-effective solutions running in the cloud or onpremise. As a leader in web and mobile manual and test automation, performance and monitoring solutions, using its Krypton framework ( open source test automation tool), along with the best available test automation tools. ThinkSys enables developers, QA Professionals and management to help reduce time to market. ThinkSys is privately held and is headquartered in Sunnyvale, CA. Since its inception in 2012, Thinksys has carved out a global niche for itself delivering top notch IT solutions in the world of Quality assurance, automation testing, Web & mobile application development. Building on to the success of its previously launched flagship open source test automation tool Krypton, which has received wholesome praise for its ingenuity and mark of approval from the entire testing community. ThinkSys has helped several Enterprises, ISVs, and Fortune 1000 companies build quality test automation suites while reducing the cost of quality by leveraging the Krypton framework.
